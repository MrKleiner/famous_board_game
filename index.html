"Who Am I?"
Who Am I? is a guessing board game where players use yes or no questions to guess the identity of a famous person who is easy to identify for anyone. This game works well with a group of any size, however the larger the group is, the more fun the game becomes.
~~
This board game's main goal and motivation is to remind these remarkable and successful people to players. Some achieve amazing things even though they have a hard life to go through. While having fun, you are also learning how and what those people did in their life. Sometimes it’s back in the past , sometimes it's in the current time.
Rules
  • DO NOT LOOK AT THE NAME ON YOUR FOREHEAD
  • WHILST PLAYING THE GAME INTERACTIVELY WITH OTHER INDIVIDUALS’, MAKE SURE YOU DON’T SEE WHO YOU ARE ON THE COMPUTER SCREEN, AS THIS WILL SPOIL THE FUN
  • IF YOU ACCIDENTALLY SEE THE ANSWER, TAKE ANOTHER CARD AND START AGAIN
  • START BY ASKING BROAD QUESTIONS
  • GET MORE SPECIFIC WITH YOUR QUESTIONS
  • STATE YOUR GUESS AS TO YOUR IDENTITY
Recommended Questions
  • ARE YOU A MALE (FEMALE)?
  • ARE YOU AN ENTERTAINER?
  • ARE YOU A SINGER (DANCER, ACTOR)?
  • ARE YOU A HISTORICAL FIGURE?
  • ARE YOU YOUNG (OLD)?
  • ARE YOU ALIVE NOW?
  • ARE YOU CONSIDERED BEAUTIFUL (HANDSOME)?
